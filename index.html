About me

Let me introduce myself

Few words about me

I'm a passionate product developer and designer with ten years experience working in all facets of the web and mobile experience.

I believe in acting quickly and failing early. I believe that there's no such thing as a right answer and believing that there is, means closing yourself and product off to incredible possibilities. I believe that done is better than perfect. I believe in measuring everything and constantly adapting and learning.

Specialized in

UI / Digital Design

I like making things pretty. I tend towards clean and simple, with few graphical elements.

UX Design

UX is not a step in the process, it is the process. UX should be hardcoded into everything you do, from design to development.

UI Development

So you have a gorgeous Sketch file and outstanding flows. Invest in the future of your app now, and architect the frontend to be as scalable and reusable as possible.
